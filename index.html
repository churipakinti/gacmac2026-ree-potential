NSERC Alliance Missions critical minerals grant

Project overview

Critical minerals grant in collaboration with industry partners, focused on REE systems in northern Saskatchewan.

Research goals

  • Investigate REE critical mineral systems and their tectonic-magmatic framework, hosted in pegmatites along the Wollaston Domain boundary in northern Saskatchewan.
  • Produce exploration toolkits to assist in the exploration of REE pegmatite resources in Saskatchewan and beyond.
  • Ore characterization (e.g., monazite) for the new Rare Earth Processing Facility in Saskatoon.

New — May 2025

NERC-NSERC UK-Canada Sustainable Critical Minerals Research Partnerships grant in collaboration with the British Geological Survey — to develop exploration and fertility indicators and a multi-method exploration toolkit.

P-Patch and Karpinka Lake pegmatites

Comparison of petrochronological characteristics from the P-Patch and Karpinka Lake pegmatites, northern Saskatchewan: what can they tell us about U and REE mineralization in the region?
