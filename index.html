Oral presentation Abstract 140

P-Patch and Karpinka Lake pegmatites

Comparison of petrochronological characteristics from the P-Patch and Karpinka Lake pegmatites, northern Saskatchewan: what can they tell us about U and REE mineralization in the region?

Petrochronological comparison of two contrasting pegmatite systems with implications for understanding uranium and rare earth element mineralization potential across northern Saskatchewan.
